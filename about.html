About Our Institution

Garden City High School is a co-educational institution located at #586 George Price Boulevard, Belmopan, Belize. We are committed to academic excellence, character development, and preparing students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our Location

Our Mission

Garden City High School is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment that inspires academic excellence, critical thinking, strong values, and lifelong learning, empowering students to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our Vision

To make Garden City High School a thriving learning community where every student grows like a well-nurtured garden developing knowledge, character, creativity, and leadership to succeed in a changing world.

Our Supportive Environment

At Garden City High School, we invite students and parents to become part of a community where innovation, discipline, and opportunity come together. Our campus isn't just a place of study; it's a fertile ground where potential is nurtured through:

  • Peer Mentorship: Older students guiding incoming First Forms.
  • Counseling Services: Emotional and academic support for every child.
  • Teacher Accessibility: Small class sizes for personalized attention.

"We don't just teach; we nurture."

Official Recognition

Garden City High School is fully recognized and registered by the Ministry of Education, Culture, Science and Technology (MOECST) of Belize.

Our curriculum is aligned with national standards to ensure our students are prepared for CXC/CSEC examinations and tertiary education.
